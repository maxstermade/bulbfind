LIGHT BULBS GRAPHIC

Shopping for light bulbs can be a difficult task - there’s always the newest technology and it’s hard for customers to keep track of why they should upgrade.  Many times, customers are overwhelmed by the options, and just bring their old bulb into the store and ask for a replacement.

Your task is to create an info-graphic to be placed on the Light Bulbs category page that will help customers understand the different options of bulbs available as well as which technology is right for them.

Be prepared to present your work and field questions during the interview.
